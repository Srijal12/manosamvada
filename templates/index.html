How it listens

Every message passes through two quiet layers

Before Manosamvada replies, it reads your message twice — once for feeling, once for safety.

◐

Emotion detection

A rule-based layer classifies each message — happy, sad, angry, anxious, neutral, or crisis — before a reply is ever drafted.

✎

Tone-matched response

The detected emotion adjusts the system's tone directive, so Meta-Llama-3.1-8B answers with the warmth or steadiness your message calls for.

✦

Crisis awareness

Messages are checked against a maintained list of crisis language. If detected, you're met with immediate support and helpline information.
